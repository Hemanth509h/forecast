# TrendCast: Heritage Business Forecasting Platform

## Project Abstract

TrendCast is a sophisticated data visualization and predictive analytics platform specifically designed for historical Indian family businesses and heritage enterprises. By bridging the gap between traditional business wisdom and modern data science, TrendCast enables legacy businesses to digitize their historical sales records and generate forward-looking insights.

The platform specializes in processing long-term historical data from diverse sectorsâ€”ranging from textile studios like Khan Tailoring to industrial giants like Tata Sons. TrendCast utilizes advanced statistical models, including Linear Regression and Moving Averages, to provide accurate sales forecasts, growth projections, and model confidence scores.

### Key Features:
- **Heritage Data Processing**: Native support for historical datasets from iconic Indian business houses.
- **Dynamic Forecasting**: Real-time generation of multi-month sales predictions using regression and seasonal analysis.
- **Intelligent Metrics**: Automated calculation of Growth Projections and Model Confidence based on data consistency.
- **AI-Powered Mapping**: Smart header mapping to streamline the import of legacy CSV formats into modern schemas.
- **Interactive Visualization**: High-fidelity charts and dashboards for exploring sales trends and regional performance.

TrendCast empowers traditional businesses to maintain their competitive edge by transforming decades of archival data into actionable intelligence for the future.
